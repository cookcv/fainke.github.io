XGBoost二阶泰勒展开式推导

发表于 2020-01-01 更新于 2020-02-01 分类于 机器学习 阅读次数： Valine：

目标函数： obj=∑i=1nl(yi,y^i(t))+∑i=1tΩ(fi)\mathrm{obj}=\sum_{i=1}^{n} l\left(y_{i}, \hat{y}_{i}^{(t)}\right)+\sum_{i=1}^{t} \Omega\left(f_{i}\right)obj=∑i=1n​l(yi​,y^​i(t)​)+∑i=1t​Ω(fi​)
其中yiy_iyi​是真实值，y^i(t)\hat{y}_{i}^{(t)}y^​i(t)​是总t棵树的预测值，lll是loss function，Ω\OmegaΩ是正则项，防止过拟合。fff是一棵CART树，ft(xi)f_{t}\left(x_{i}\right)ft​(xi​)是输入数据xix_ixi​后第t棵树的输出值。
y^i(t)\hat{y}_{i}^{(t)}y^​i(t)​与ft(xi)f_{t}\left(x_{i}\right)ft​(xi​)的关系为：
y^i(t)=∑k=1tfk(xi)=y^i(t−1)+ft(xi)\hat{y}_{i}^{(t)}=\sum_{k=1}^{t} f_{k}\left(x_{i}\right)=\hat{y}_{i}^{(t-1)}+f_{t}\left(x_{i}\right)y^​i(t)​=∑k=1t​fk​(xi​)=y^​i(t−1)​+ft​(xi​)
因此，目标函数即可转为：
obj(t)=∑i=1nl(yi,y^i(t))+∑i=1tΩ(fi)=∑i=1nl(yi,y^i(t−1)+ft(xi))+Ω(ft)+constant \begin{aligned} \mathrm{obj}^{(t)} &=\sum_{i=1}^{n} l\left(y_{i}, \hat{y}_{i}^{(t)}\right)+\sum_{i=1}^{t} \Omega\left(f_{i}\right) \\ &=\sum_{i=1}^{n} l\left(y_{i}, \hat{y}_{i}^{(t-1)}+f_{t}\left(x_{i}\right)\right)+\Omega\left(f_{t}\right)+\text {constant } \end{aligned}obj(t)​=i=1∑n​l(yi​,y^​i(t)​)+i=1∑t​Ω(fi​)=i=1∑n​l(yi​,y^​i(t−1)​+ft​(xi​))+Ω(ft​)+constant ​

泰勒展开公式： f(x+Δx)≃f(x)+f′(x)Δx+12f′′(x)Δx2f(x+\Delta x) \simeq f(x)+f^{\prime}(x) \Delta x+\frac{1}{2} f^{\prime \prime}(x) \Delta x^{2}f(x+Δx)≃f(x)+f′(x)Δx+21​f′′(x)Δx2
将损失函数泰勒展开至二阶：
obj(t)=∑i=1n[l(yi,y^i(t−1))+gift(xi)+12hift2(xi)]+Ω(ft)+constant\mathrm{obj}^{(t)}=\sum_{i=1}^{n}\left[l\left(y_{i}, \hat{y}_{i}^{(t-1)}\right)+g_{i} f_{t}\left(x_{i}\right)+\frac{1}{2} h_{i} f_{t}^{2}\left(x_{i}\right)\right]+\Omega\left(f_{t}\right)+constantobj(t)=∑i=1n​[l(yi​,y^​i(t−1)​)+gi​ft​(xi​)+21​hi​ft2​(xi​)]+Ω(ft​)+constant
其中gi=∂y^(t−1)l(yi,y^(t−1))g_{i}=\partial_{\hat{y}^{(t-1)}} l\left(y_{i}, \hat{y}^{(t-1)}\right)gi​=∂y^​(t−1)​l(yi​,y^​(t−1))，hi=∂y^(t−1)2l(yi,y^(t−1))h_{i}=\partial_{\hat{y}^{(t-1)}}^{2} l\left(y_{i}, \hat{y}^{(t-1)}\right)hi​=∂y^​(t−1)2​l(yi​,y^​(t−1))
分别表示对y^i(t−1)\hat{y}_{i}^{(t-1)}y^​i(t−1)​求一次与二次偏导。
与泰勒展开式对应来看，其中x为y^i(t−1)\hat{y}_{i}^{(t-1)}y^​i(t−1)​，Δx\Delta xΔx为ft(xi)f_{t}\left(x_{i}\right)ft​(xi​)
